SSAS Partition and Incremental Refresh Strategy

1) Partitioning key
- Partition fact tables by EventDate (daily partitions).
- Optional sub-partition by LineCode for large plants.

2) Retention policy
- Keep daily partitions for 90 days.
- Merge into monthly partitions after day 90.

3) Refresh cadence
- Hot window: every 15 minutes for today and yesterday.
- Warm window: hourly for previous 7 days.
- Cold window: daily for older partitions if corrections are expected.

4) Late-arriving data
- Keep a correction window of last 3 days in each refresh run.
- Reprocess impacted partitions when late rows are detected.

5) Validation
- Compare KPI parity with SQL baseline queries after each refresh.
- Alert if KPI drift exceeds threshold (for example, abs delta > 0.3%).
