SSRS and Crystal Expression Suggestions

1) Percent formatting
- Expression: =Format(Fields!RejectPct.Value / 100, "P2")
- Use for display only; do not compute in report if SQL already returns the metric.

2) Severity indicator text
- Expression: =IIF(Fields!MaxSeverity.Value >= 4, "Critical", IIF(Fields!MaxSeverity.Value >= 2, "Warning", "Info"))

3) Null-safe downtime display
- Expression: =IIF(IsNothing(Fields!DowntimeMinutes.Value), 0, Fields!DowntimeMinutes.Value)

4) Shift label
- Expression: =Fields!ShiftName.Value & " (" & Format(Fields!ShiftStart.Value, "HH:mm") & " - " & Format(Fields!ShiftEnd.Value, "HH:mm") & ")"

5) OEE quality flag
- Expression: =IIF(Fields!OEEPct.Value < 0.65, "Investigate", "Within target")
